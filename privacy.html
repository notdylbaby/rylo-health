Privacy policy.

Last updated April 28, 2026 · Full policy being finalized

Your information, in our care.

Rylo Health is committed to protecting your privacy and handling your personal and health information with the highest standard of care. We comply with the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA) and all applicable federal and state privacy laws.

Our full Privacy Policy is currently being finalized by our legal team. It will cover how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information, including how we share data with licensed providers, compounding pharmacies, and third-party service providers under appropriate Business Associate Agreements.

We do not sell your data. Your health information is used solely to provide you with medical care through our platform.

What we collect.

When you reserve early access or join the Founders' Circle, we collect basic contact information — your email address, ZIP code, and product interest. If you become a patient at launch, we will collect additional medical and identity information necessary to provide care, in accordance with HIPAA.

How we use it.

Your information is used to (1) notify you when Rylo Health opens for enrollment, (2) connect you with licensed healthcare providers if you become a patient, (3) coordinate prescription fulfillment with licensed compounding pharmacies, and (4) communicate with you about your care.

We do not share your information with advertisers, data brokers, or any third party for marketing purposes.

Your rights.

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information at any time by contacting us. Patients in California, Virginia, Colorado, and other states with applicable privacy laws have additional rights under those statutes, which we will honor.

Questions or concerns

Email hello@rylohealth.com — we'll respond within one business day.
