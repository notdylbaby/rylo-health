Compounded medications

Medications dispensed through Rylo Health's pharmacy partners are compounded medications and are not FDA-approved drug products. Compounding is legal and regulated under federal and state pharmacy law, but compounded drugs have not undergone FDA review for safety, efficacy, or manufacturing quality.

Individual results vary. Clinical statistics referenced on this website are sourced from published trials of FDA-approved branded formulations and cannot be directly extrapolated to compounded equivalents.
